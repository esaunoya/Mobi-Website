Social Coding

Social Coding is held almost every Friday during the Fall and Spring semesters (see calendar). It's a time where students that know a little bit about a framework, language, or platform can team up and create a fun and exciting project

Join the Team

Join Social Coding! We'll be working together on a project next meeting. You don't have to be a pro to join social coding. Just know the basics of git and have a little background on iOS, Android, Unity or web development. We'll give you some resources to help! Every Social Coding project allows for leadership opurtunities, expirence, and service.
