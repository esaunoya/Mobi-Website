FAQ

How can I join?

Becoming a memeber means getting a t-shirt and access to the member dashboard. To join Mobi it's $15 (sorry we only take cash right now). Speak to one of our officers at the end of a Mobi Learn Workshop.

Do I have to be a member to attend Mobi Learn Workshops?

Nope! All our workshops are free to University of Texas at Arlington Students.

I'm not a Computer Science/Computer Engineering/Software Engineering Major. Can I still join?

Of course! Any student of any major can join Mobi and attend workshops.

I don't know how to code.

That is prefectly fine! Our workshops are for beginners.

I have an idea for start-up. Can you guys help me create it?

Unfortunatly we can only create projects for Social Coding. But if you have a start-up idea you want to presue on your own and need guidance check out the UTA start-up lounge
